## Admin User Story
The administrator logs into the account. They can manage the doctors, patients booking/appointments etc. They have a general overview of the whole system and can 
manage all users in the system. 

## Patient User Story
A patient logs in. They can book an appointment with a doctor, they can see their prescriptions and they can also manage their account.

## Doctor User Story. 
A doctor views the appointments they have and can also give prescriptions.
